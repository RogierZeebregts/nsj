Mississippi Square

Here, you’ll find oysters, satay, fried fish, Indian curries, falafel, crepes, among others.

Hudson Square

Right from the start in 2018 the new square with lots of seating has proven itself. Hudson Square has French fries, the Bourgondier with croquettes, and the hamburger truck has expanded even more with a larger assortment. Also present is a truck with Vietnamese specials where you can buy Peking duck and a truck with fish soup and poké bowls.
